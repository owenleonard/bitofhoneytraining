Dream

13.1 hands, year old APHA/AQHA grade mare. Cute, stocky sorrel mare with big blaze and sock on left front. Lightly started under saddle, good with english or western tack, goes bitless or in a snaffle. Excellent ground manners, loads in trailer, ground ties, stands quietly for farrier and vet. Affectionate and likes dogs and kids. Ground drives but never hitched to a cart. Cute mover, forward and balanced. Would be great as a kids pony with slightly more training, or perfect for a petite adult. Sound sound sound! No history of lameness or health issues. Sadly, lack of time forces sale. $1000 neg to approved home only.

Doits Cat, (barn name Dewey)

**Contact Kim to schedule an appointment to see Dewey for the first week of March at Bit of Honey Training to try him before the expo!**

Dewey is a Jockey Club registered 16h dark bay year old Thoroughbred gelding. Never raced, low mileage! He is beautiful, quiet, sensitive, and eager to please. He was in race training but never made it to the starting gate. In July 2014 he went into CANTER Colorado's aftercare program and was let down from race training with them. He has grown quite a bit in a short period of time!

Starting December 2014, he joined Kim Leonard with Bit of Honey Training to train for the 2015 Equine Comeback Challenge, organized by A Home for Every Horse. Kim and Dewey will show off their skills together in March at the horse expo. Dewey will then be available to purchase at the Rocky Mountain Horse Expo, price upon request.
