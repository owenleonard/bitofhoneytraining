Saddle Fitting

Kim has years of experience with fitting many types of saddles to different horses. Often behavioral problems and lameness stem from ill-fitting tack. When the saddle is comfortable, the horse is comfortable. For more specific details about how a saddle fits a moving horse, we use the Port Lewis Saddle Fit pad, a transparent saddle pad filled with a red play-dough substance. It does not correct anything; it is a superb diagnostic tool. The horse is tacked up wearing normal equipment, including the impression pad. After the play-dough is warmed up and thus displaced by pressure points, it shows very clearly where fitting corrections need to be made. Whether the need is for a specific style of Western, English, or Australian saddle, Kim can assist you in finding the right fit for you and your horse.
